Soldering Tempco resistors.

1.

Tempco resistors have to be placed on top of each LM3046 chips.
There is little chance for the resistors legs to be in contact with a soldering point on the Analog Board. In case you are worried, you can either wrap the legs with some heatshrink or apply some silicon.
